## What problems does an onboarding checklist solve?

Employee onboarding usually involves HR, direct managers, IT, and trainers at the same time. The to-do items may include device preparation, account provisioning, security setup, employee handbook confirmation, team introductions, and role training. When these tasks are scattered across emails, chat threads, and spreadsheets, things get missed, delayed, or left without a clear owner.

With this system, you can create an independent onboarding profile for each new hire, recording their name, position, department, direct manager, email, and start date, and configure a complete onboarding task checklist for them.

Tasks can be organized into stages such as pre-boarding, week one, week two, and the first month. Each task can have a category, an owner, a due date, and a current status. Employees and managers can directly see which tasks are completed, in progress, or not started, as well as the overall onboarding completion rate.

The system also lets you quickly switch between employees, making it easy for HR or managers to compare onboarding progress across new hires and spot unfinished tasks in time.

## Core features

### Employee onboarding profiles

- **New hire basics:** Keep name, position, department, email, start date, and current onboarding status in one place.
- **Direct manager link:** Associate each new hire with a direct manager so the primary owner of the onboarding process is clear.
- **Profile switching:** Switch between different new hires to view each one's tasks and completion progress.

### Staged onboarding plans

- **Stage-based task organization:** Organize checklist items by pre-boarding, week one, week two, and the first month.
- **Stage progress display:** Track task counts and completion per stage to quickly identify unfinished areas.
- **Flexible plan adjustments:** Configure different onboarding plans for different employees based on position, department, and start date.

### Onboarding tasks and collaboration

- **Task information management:** Record task title, category, owner, due date, and current status.
- **Multi-role task assignment:** Assign tasks like device preparation, account provisioning, policy introductions, and training to HR, IT, managers, or trainers.
- **Task status tracking:** Use statuses such as not started, in progress, and completed to clearly show how each task is going.

### Onboarding progress overview

- **Key metrics:** Show total tasks, completed count, in-progress count, and overall completion rate.
- **Per-employee progress:** View each new hire's full onboarding journey and catch missed or overdue tasks in time.
- **Task detail view:** See task category, owner, due date, status, and update time in one place for easy follow-up.

### AI-powered assistance

- **AI-generated onboarding highlights:** Summarize what a new hire should focus on early based on their position and department.
- **AI-recommended checklist items:** Automatically generate suggested tasks like role responsibilities, department introductions, tool setup, and system access.
- **AI-generated training plans:** Fill in initial training, key collaborators, and stage goals to help HR quickly create personalized onboarding checklists.

## FAQ

1. **How is this different from a regular to-do list or an onboarding tool generated by vibe coding?**

A regular to-do list usually only records task names and completion status, and vibe coding is better suited to quickly producing a form or a task page. Once the system is actually used inside a company, you also need employee profiles, onboarding stages, task categories, owners, due dates, permissions, operation history, and long-term maintainability.

NocoBase organizes employees, tasks, and onboarding progress in one system, and lets you enable workflows, permission management, audit logging, single sign-on, and plugin extensions as needed, so you don't have to repeatedly bolt on basic enterprise capabilities later.

1. **Can different employees have different onboarding tasks?**

Yes. Every employee can have their own onboarding task checklist. Teams can arrange different tasks, stages, owners, and due dates based on each employee's position, department, and start date.

For example, engineers can get extra development environment and account setup tasks, while marketers can get brand, content, and channel training tasks.

1. **Can AI generate an onboarding plan based on an employee's position and department?**

Yes. AI can generate onboarding highlights and suggested checklist items based on a new hire's position and department — such as role goals, department introductions, tool setup, collaborators, and training tasks — which HR or managers review before adding to the checklist.

1. **Can tasks be organized by onboarding stage?**

Yes. Tasks can be organized into stages such as pre-boarding, week one, week two, and the first month. Employees, HR, and direct managers can clearly see each stage's tasks, owners, due dates, and completion status.

You can also add stages later — like probation reviews, 30-day check-ins, or 90-day evaluations — to match your company's actual process.

1. **Can I control who can view and edit which onboarding information?**

Yes. NocoBase supports role-based permissions with data scopes.

For example:

- New hires can only view and update their own tasks;
- HR can maintain employee profiles and all onboarding plans;
- Direct managers can view their reports' onboarding progress;
- IT and training leads can only handle tasks assigned to them;
- Administrators can configure fields, pages, flows, and permissions.

1. **Can it integrate with our existing company login system?**

Yes, on demand. NocoBase supports connecting to enterprise identity and single sign-on systems via the corresponding plugins, so employees keep signing in with their existing company accounts.

After login, the system controls which employee profiles, onboarding tasks, and admin pages each user can see based on their role, with no separate account system to maintain for onboarding.

1. **Can it record operation history and audit logs for onboarding tasks?**

Yes, enable it as needed. The system can keep records of task status changes, owner reassignments, field edits, and completion times, helping HR trace who completed or modified a task and when.

For critical onboarding items like account provisioning, device handover, and security training, audit and operation history also provide a clear record of accountability.

1. **Can an onboarding system built with AI be adjusted over the long term?**

Yes. What the AI agent generates is the system's foundation, not a one-off static page.

After going live, you can keep adjusting employee fields, onboarding stages, task categories, owner rules, page layouts, workflows, permissions, and plugin capabilities to fit the ever-changing onboarding processes of different departments, roles, and company policies.

1. **Is this system ready for real internal company use?**

Yes. NocoBase already provides the data models, permission management, workflows, operation history, audit logging, single sign-on, notifications, APIs, and plugin extensions that internal enterprise systems commonly need, all available on demand.

Teams can start by quickly building the core onboarding checklist with an AI agent, then gradually add approvals, reminders, account management, and system integrations — a better fit for long-term maintenance than redoing everything after each requirement change, as with typical vibe coding.
