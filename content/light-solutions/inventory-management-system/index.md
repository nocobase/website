## What problems does an inventory management system solve?

An inventory management system helps teams deal with scattered stock data, error-prone manual spreadsheets, delayed stock updates, and low-stock situations discovered too late.

With this system you can centrally record product basics, current stock, categories, storage locations and safety stock levels. When stock falls below the safety threshold, the system automatically raises a low-stock alert so the team can restock in time.

Managers can also use the dashboard to check total inventory value, product counts, low-stock items, stock trends and category distribution — and judge inventory health at a glance.

## FAQ

- **How is this different from an inventory system generated by plain vibe coding?**

Plain vibe coding is great for quick prototypes, but internal business systems also need permissions, workflows, audit logs, operation history, SSO, plugin extensions and long-term maintainability. NocoBase already provides these foundations, so the AI agent builds on top of them instead of trial-and-erroring from scratch.

- **Can an AI-built inventory system be iterated on long-term?**

Yes. NocoBase is not a one-shot prototype generator but a maintainable business-system platform. After generation you can keep adjusting collections, fields, pages, filters, workflows, permissions and plugins.

- **Can I control who gets access to what?**

Yes. NocoBase ships with role-based access control: warehouse staff can be limited to stock records, managers to dashboards, and admins to system structure and permission rules.

- **Can I set up automated workflows?**

Yes. NocoBase workflows can automate inventory tasks such as low-stock alerts, stock record updates, adjustment notifications, approvals and scheduled checks — the system drives the process instead of just recording data.

- **Is it ready for serious internal use?**

Yes. NocoBase provides permissions, workflows, audit logs, operation history, SSO, notifications, dashboards, file management, APIs and plugin extensions — enable what you need. Compared with one-off vibe-coded prototypes, it is built for maintainable, extensible, long-lived systems.

- **Can it send low-stock reminders?**

Yes. Set a safety stock level per product and a workflow will alert the right people whenever stock drops below it.

- **Does it support multiple warehouses?**

Yes. Products can carry warehouse, bin location and stock records, and you can view quantities and movements per warehouse.

- **Can I adapt fields and pages to my own business?**

Yes. NocoBase supports custom collections, fields, pages, filters, dashboards and flows — built for systems you keep maintaining.
