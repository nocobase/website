## What Is an Audit Management System?

An audit management system is an AI business system for internal audit, finance, compliance, IT, and management teams. It is used to centrally manage audit findings, evidence materials, owners, remediation deadlines, and risk status. Teams can first let an AI Agent generate fields, issue lists, remediation pages, charts, and timelines based on the audit process, then continue adjusting data models, role permissions, status flows, and audit records in NocoBase, extending the system from audit issue registration to remediation tracking and review management. The example system can uniformly track 12 audit findings, 6 AI-identified issues, and 5 overdue remediation tasks. After setup, NocoBase AI employees can also extract evidence information, analyze audit risks, organize remediation suggestions, and assist with multilingual translation and form filling.

## Core Value

* 🔍 **Centrally track 12 audit findings, 6 of which are identified and classified by AI**
* ⏰ **Identify 5 overdue remediation tasks and expose issues that have not been handled on time**
* 📊 **Manage audit risks across 4 levels: critical, high, medium, and low**
* 🤖 **Achieve an average AI evidence analysis confidence level of 89%, reducing the time spent manually organizing audit materials**

## System Preview

### 1.Audit Findings and Remediation Overview

The audit overview centrally displays open issues, overdue remediation items, AI-identified issues, and the overall resolution rate. Auditors can view each finding by issue number, severity, control area, and remediation status. They can also use AI evidence analysis to quickly determine whether the materials are sufficient, which issues remain unresolved, and which fields require manual review.

![Audit findings and remediation overview](https://static-docs.nocobase.com/Audit%20Management1-7bkjtn.png)

### 2.Risk Distribution and Audit Activity Records

The system can count audit findings by control areas such as access control, change management, data protection, vendor risk, financial close, and physical security. It can also display the distribution of issues by severity. Audit activity records retain actions such as AI identification, system record creation, owner assignment, and evidence review, making it easier for the team to track how each issue is handled.

![Risk distribution and audit activity records](https://static-docs.nocobase.com/Audit%20Management2-oyukg8.png)

### 3.Linked Evidence and Remediation Timeline

Each audit finding can be linked to evidence files such as PDFs and Excel documents, with file type, content description, and classification results recorded. The system also keeps a remediation timeline, showing key actions such as issue discovery, material supplementation, manual review, and final resolution, so audit evidence and the remediation process can correspond to each other.

![Linked evidence and remediation timeline](https://static-docs.nocobase.com/Audit%20Management3-n1nz9m.png)

### 4.Audit Finding Details and AI Assessment

The audit detail page centrally displays the issue number, title, severity, status, control area, control number, remediation owner, target date, inherent risk, and AI confidence level. NocoBase AI employees can generate risk assessments and remediation suggestions based on the issue description and evidence materials, helping auditors more quickly determine the cause, impact scope, and next steps.

![Audit finding details and AI assessment](https://static-docs.nocobase.com/Audit%20Management4-1wrhmd.png)

### 5.AI Multilingual Translation and Form Filling

For cross-region audits and international teams, NocoBase AI employees can translate issue titles, descriptions, control numbers, analysis conclusions, and remediation suggestions into the target language and fill them directly into the audit form. People only need to confirm the translation results and professional terminology, reducing the work of copying, translating, and entering items one by one.

![AI multilingual translation and form filling](https://static-docs.nocobase.com/Audit%20Management5-8nouui.png)

## Applicable Scenarios

## FAQ

### 1.Which teams is an audit management system suitable for?

It is suitable for internal audit, risk control, information security, financial compliance, vendor management, and legal teams, especially enterprises that need to continuously track audit findings and remediation processes.

### 2.What audit information can the system manage?

It can manage audit finding numbers, titles, severity, control areas, status, remediation owners, target dates, risk levels, evidence files, AI assessments, and remediation suggestions.

### 3.Does it support linking audit evidence?

Yes. Each audit finding can be linked to evidence files such as PDFs and Excel documents, with file descriptions, classification results, and review status recorded for later review.

### 4.Does it support AI analysis of audit issues?

Yes. **NocoBase AI employees** can extract evidence information, analyze issue risks, generate remediation suggestions, and display confidence levels. Auditors can focus on reviewing low-confidence and high-risk content.

### 5.Why build an audit management system with AI + NocoBase?

Teams can describe audit fields, pages, and remediation workflows to an AI Agent in natural language, and the Agent can call NocoBase components to complete system setup. NocoBase handles data relationships, permissions, views, and process management, while NocoBase AI employees handle evidence analysis, risk judgment, content translation, and form filling, making it more suitable for continuously running enterprise audit management scenarios.
