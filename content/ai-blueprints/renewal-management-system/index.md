## What is a renewal management system?

A renewal management system helps teams manage customer contract expirations, renewal progress, customer health, and renewal risk. With an **AI Agent connected to NocoBase**, teams can quickly generate core pages such as renewal opportunities, customer records, and renewal dashboards. Users only need to confirm the field structure, page logic, and business rules, so a basic system can be built in as little as **about one hour**, without relying on scattered Excel sheets, calendar reminders, and sales notes.

It is useful for sales, customer success, and customer operations teams that manage SaaS subscriptions, B2B contract renewals, ARR risk, and customer health. Instead of keeping renewal work in fragmented records, the system turns it into a clear, continuous, and collaborative management process.

## Core value

* ⏱️ **Bring renewal information into one system to reduce cross-sheet lookup and duplicate maintenance**
* 💰 **Track US\$2.33M in open renewal ARR and US\$1.05M in at-risk ARR in real time**
* 📊 **Move renewals through stages such as upcoming, outreach, negotiation, and risk warning**

The system centralizes every renewal opportunity, including the customer, product, owner, renewal ARR, uplift percentage, win probability, expiration date, and current stage. Teams can quickly see which renewals are moving forward, which ones are already at risk, and how much revenue is tied to each opportunity.

## FAQ

### 1.What teams is a renewal management system suitable for?

It is suitable for SaaS sales teams, customer success teams, customer operations teams, and account management teams, especially in scenarios that involve subscription renewals, annual contract renewals, and ARR risk management.

### 2.Can the system track renewal revenue and renewal probability?

Yes. The system can record renewal revenue, uplift percentage, win probability, and current stage, helping teams evaluate the revenue value, progress, and likelihood of each renewal opportunity.

### 3.Does it support upcoming expiration reminders?

Yes. Teams can use the renewal list, stage board, and calendar view to identify customers with upcoming expirations and arrange outreach and follow-up in advance.

### 4.Does it support AI queries and renewal analysis?

Yes. Teams can use **NocoBase AI Employee** to query system data directly, such as the number and percentage of upcoming expirations, the distribution of at-risk customers, and renewal progress. Instead of manually filtering spreadsheets, teams can quickly obtain renewal analysis for daily management, weekly reporting, and renewal reviews.

### 5.Why build a renewal management system with AI + NocoBase?

AI can help quickly generate the field structure, page descriptions, analysis questions, and renewal statistics logic. NocoBase provides the data relationships, permissions, views, workflows, and extension capabilities needed for long-term use, making it more suitable for internal business systems than relying only on Excel or temporary AI tools.
