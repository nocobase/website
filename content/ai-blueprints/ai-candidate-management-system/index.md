## What is a candidate management system?

A candidate management system is an AI-powered business system for recruiting, HR, and interview collaboration. It helps teams manage candidate information, job matching, resume parsing, interview scheduling, and scoring results. Teams can describe the recruiting workflow and page structure they need in natural language, and an **AI Agent** can call **NocoBase** tables, forms, detail pages, calendars, boards, and scoring components to build the system.

After the basic structure is in place, **NocoBase AI employees** can continue extracting information from resumes, such as names, job titles, years of experience, companies, email addresses, and candidate sources. In this example, the system can manage **9 candidates, 5 recruiting stages, multiple job openings, and several interview scoring dimensions**, making the process from resume collection to interview evaluation clearer and easier to maintain.

## FAQ

### 1.What teams is a candidate management system suitable for?

It is suitable for HR teams, recruiting teams, startups, technical teams, and recruiting agencies, especially when they need to manage multiple positions, candidates, and interview rounds at the same time.

### 2.Can the system parse resumes automatically?

Yes. NocoBase AI employees can extract information such as names, job titles, years of experience, current companies, email addresses, and candidate sources from resumes, then fill the results into the corresponding fields.

### 3.Does it support interview scoring?

Yes. The system can record interview scores across dimensions such as technical depth, problem solving, communication, and culture fit. It can also store interviewer notes on strengths, risks, and final recommendations.

### 4.Can it manage recruiting workflows for different roles?

Yes. Candidates can be linked to different roles and moved through stages such as screening, onsite interview, review, offer, hired, and rejected, making it suitable for parallel hiring across multiple positions.

### 5.Why build a candidate management system with AI + NocoBase?

AI Agents can use natural language instructions to call NocoBase components and build candidate tables, resume forms, interview rooms, and scoring pages. NocoBase provides the data structure, permissions, views, and extension capabilities needed for long-term use. Combined with NocoBase AI employees for resume extraction and information organization, it is easier to maintain than a recruiting process spread across Excel, email, and folders.
