## What is a contract management system?

A contract management system is an internal business system for managing contract records, renewals, key clauses, obligations, and risk information in one place. It is designed for legal, procurement, finance, and business teams that need to work from the same contract data.

In this demo, teams can review 20 contracts, 9 contracts expiring within 60 days, 12 pending obligations, and \$5.3M in auto-renewal contract value. You can also use AI Agents such as Claude Code, Codex, Cursor, or OpenCode to generate the initial structure, then continue refining fields, pages, permissions, and workflows in NocoBase so the prototype can become a long-term contract management system.

The system brings contract portfolio data, contracts expiring within 60 days, auto-renewal value, pending obligations, and average risk score into one view. ### 2.AI contract activity and obligation tasks

NocoBase AI employees can record contract-related actions, such as extracting key fields, identifying indemnity clause risks, classifying new contracts, drafting renewal notes, and finding auto-renewal dates. The obligation list helps teams track renewal notices, indemnity reviews, CPI adjustments, conditional investigations, auto-renewal opt-out reminders, and other follow-up tasks, reducing the risk that responsibilities are forgotten after a contract is signed.

### 3.Contract details and risk clauses

The contract detail page shows the contract name, contract ID, counterparty, contract type, status, annual value, owner, signing date, term, notice period, risk score, governing law, key clauses, and AI confidence. Legal and business teams can use the same page to decide whether a contract needs renewal, review, or further approval.

![Contract details and risk clauses in NocoBase](https://static-docs.nocobase.com/Contract%20Management3-7ec8ep.png)

### 4.AI contract extraction and form filling

NocoBase AI employees can extract the contract name, ID, counterparty, type, status, owner, signing date, term, notice period, risk score, and key clauses from contract text, then fill them into the contract form. The human role is mainly to verify the extracted results, add business judgment, and submit the record, reducing manual entry and missed clauses.

## FAQ

**1.What teams is a contract management system suitable for?**

It is suitable for legal, procurement, finance, operations, and management teams, especially companies that need to manage many contracts, renewal dates, obligations, and risk clauses at the same time.

**2.What types of contracts can the system manage?**

It can manage common business contracts such as lease agreements, service contracts, software licenses, NDAs, master service agreements, service level agreements, and data processing agreements.

**3.Does it support contract expiration and auto-renewal reminders?**

Yes. The system can record contract expiration dates, notice periods, auto-renewal status, and remaining days, then use the calendar and obligation tasks to remind teams to act earlier.

**4.Does it support AI contract information extraction?**

Yes. **NocoBase AI employees** can extract contract names, IDs, counterparties, terms, notice periods, key clauses, risk scores, and AI confidence from contract text, then fill the data into the contract form.

**5.Why build a contract management system with AI and NocoBase?**

AI Agents can use natural language to call NocoBase components and build contract records, detail pages, lifecycle calendars, and obligation lists. NocoBase provides the data relationships, permissions, views, and workflow extensions needed for long-term use. Together with NocoBase AI employees for contract extraction, risk detection, and task generation, it is better suited for ongoing contract operations than relying only on folders, Excel, and email.
