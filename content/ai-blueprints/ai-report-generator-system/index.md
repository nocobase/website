## What is an AI report generator?

An AI report generator is an AI business system for consolidating business data, extracting key findings, and generating readable, shareable data analysis reports. Teams can describe the required fields, pages, and report workflow in natural language, then let an AI Agent create the report list, detail pages, data tables, and output pages. NocoBase AI employees can then read the data, generate summaries, identify risks, and organize charts. In this example, the system manages 17 reports, 5 business topics, and 4 report formats, with an average report confidence score of 87.7%. It turns manual report preparation into a reusable internal analytics workflow.

Each report can store its title, business category, status, delivery format, owner, page count, generation date, and confidence score. Reviewers can see which data was used, what conclusions the AI produced, and which parts still need confirmation.

Report conclusions can be broken down into structured findings and metrics. Teams can record the finding level, specific insight, supporting notes, metric name, metric value, and confidence score. The report list manages title, business category, generation status, report format, owner, confidence score, page count, and date in one place. ## Key features

### 1.Report task management

Maintain report title, business category, owner, status, delivery format, page count, generation date, and confidence score in one place, so teams can track each report from production to review.

### 2.AI summaries and key finding extraction

NocoBase AI employees can generate report summaries from business data, extract positive signals, risk indicators, abnormal changes, and key metrics, then save those conclusions as structured records.

### 3.Confidence and review management

The system records confidence scores for reports and key metrics, separating high-, medium-, and low-confidence results. Content with low confidence or incomplete data can be moved into a review status for business users to confirm before distribution.

### 4.Chart generation and multi-format output

AI can generate charts for categories, status, trends, and metrics based on report data. Completed reports can be previewed online and exported as Markdown, HTML, or PDF. They can also be managed as full reports, executive summaries, email briefs, or presentations.


## FAQ

### 1.What teams is an AI report generator suitable for?

It is suitable for operations, finance, procurement, marketing, product, security, and management teams, especially companies that need to generate weekly reports, monthly reports, business analysis, and management briefs on a regular basis.

### 2.What types of reports can the system generate?

It can be used for business analysis, procurement spend, cloud cost optimization, brand sentiment, product adoption, sales forecasting, security incident reviews, and customer churn risk reports.

### 3.Do AI-generated reports need human review?

A review step is recommended. The system shows each report’s confidence score and status. High-confidence reports can be confirmed quickly, while reports with incomplete data or uncertain conclusions can stay in a pending review status.

### 4.Can it analyze existing report data?

Yes. **NocoBase AI employees** can read report records in the system, analyze report categories, generation status, average confidence score, and pending tasks, then generate an overall analysis report with summaries and charts.

### 5.Why build a report generator with AI + NocoBase?

Teams can describe report fields, management pages, and generation workflows to an AI Agent in natural language, and the Agent can call NocoBase components to build the system. NocoBase stores the data, manages permissions, configures views, and supports long-term expansion, while NocoBase AI employees handle data analysis, content extraction, and report generation. This makes it suitable for enterprise reporting workflows that need to run continuously.
